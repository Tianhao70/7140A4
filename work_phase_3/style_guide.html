Style Guide: Global Roots Community

Welcome to the definitive Style Guide for the "Global Roots Community" design proposal (Work Phase 3). This guide serves as the foundational blueprint for creating a consistent, accessible, engaging, and supportive online experience tailored for Australia's international communities. It meticulously details every aspect of our design language—from the immediate visual appeal (Visceral) and intuitive usability (Behavioural) to the profound emotional connection and cultural resonance (Reflective) we aim to foster.

Our design philosophy is deeply rooted in user-centered principles. We are committed to exceeding WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility standards, integrating ethical considerations at every step, and championing cultural sensitivity. Through this Style Guide, we lay the groundwork to ensure the "Global Roots Community" website genuinely empowers its diverse users, simplifies their settlement journey, and cultivates a strong, enduring sense of belonging.

Visceral Design

Focuses on the immediate aesthetic appeal – the look and feel. This section covers page layouts, typography choices, color schemes, and imagery guidelines that define the website's visual identity and first impression.

Behavioural Design

Concerns the usability, function, and overall user experience during interaction. This includes navigation patterns, interactive components like buttons and forms, and feedback mechanisms ensuring ease of use.

Reflective Design

Relates to the user's long-term reflections, the conveyed message, and emotional connection. Addresses overall appeal, cultural meaning, ethical considerations, trust, and how the design supports user values.
