Explore the Design Proposal

Our Design Journey (Work Phase 3)

This interactive website presents the comprehensive Design Proposal for the "Global Roots Community" platform. Rooted in the insights from our Work Phase 2 research—which included in-depth analysis of client needs, user persona interviews, and the development of key user stories—this proposal articulates a holistic design system.

We showcase how Visceral design elements (appealing layouts, harmonious colors, clear typography), Behavioural interaction patterns (intuitive navigation, accessible components), and Reflective design considerations (core values, emotional resonance, ethical practices) are interwoven. Our goal is to create a cohesive, accessible (WCAG 2.2 AA compliant), and empowering user experience that directly addresses the needs of international students and newcomers in Australia, as highlighted by our A2 personas: Shihao Wu, Rajesh Kumar, Nguyen Van Cuong, and Isabella Rossi.

Key Pillars of the "Global Roots Community"

Our design proposal focuses on creating tangible solutions for several core platform areas, meticulously crafted to support our users:

Vibrant Community Hub

A central space for peer-to-peer support through discussions (Topics, Posts), sharing practical resources (Settlement, Jobs), and navigating student life (Study Apply, College Life).

Inclusive Accessibility

Prioritizing features like language support options, adjustable visual settings, auditory assistance, and full keyboard navigation, all grounded in WCAG 2.2 AA standards.

Trust & Information

Transparent "About Us" section to build credibility, clear "Contact Us" channels, and secure, user-friendly "Log In/Sign Up" systems for personalized and safe interaction.

Our Guiding Vision

The ultimate aim of the "Global Roots Community" is to cultivate a profound sense of belonging and to significantly empower newcomers. We envision a reliable, accessible, and deeply supportive online environment, meticulously tailored to address their specific needs during the often-challenging phases of settlement and integration into Australian life. This vision is directly informed by our A2 research and steadfastly supports the client's mission to create meaningful connections and provide an inclusive, ethically designed digital platform.
