WP2 Client Needs Analysis

The Client Brief

Project Overview

Our project delivers a dedicated online platform designed to support and connect Australia's vibrant international communities. Recognizing the challenges faced by international students, new migrants, and their families when settling into a new environment, this website serves as a central, trustworthy hub. Our goal is to empower newcomers and foster a greater sense of belonging and social cohesion.

We provide practical settlement guidance, resources for employment and daily life, and pathways to build meaningful connections within specific cultural groups and the broader Australian community. Aligned with our mission, the platform aims to celebrate cultural diversity, ensure information is accessible, and create a welcoming, inclusive, and ethically designed space for all users. This initiative directly addresses the need for reliable, peer-informed support to navigate life in Australia successfully.

Community Focus Areas

Shaped by community needs, our key focus areas are:

  • Settling In: Practical guidance on housing, banking, transport & daily essentials.
  • Employment: Resources for job seeking, career growth & networking.
  • Connection: Finding cultural groups, events & building support networks.
  • Information Hub: A central place for reliable info & shared experiences.

Target Audience

This website aims to serve three primary groups within the 'Regional or International Communities' focus:

  1. International Students: Seeking academic support, practical settlement advice (housing, banking), peer connections, and resources for navigating student life in Australia. (Reflecting insights similar to Shihao Wu's interview).
  2. New Immigrants/Migrants: Including skilled workers and families (like Rajesh Kumar, Nguyen Van Cuong, Isabella Rossi), needing localized information on employment, family settlement (schools, partner jobs), healthcare, language support, and connection with both their cultural community and the broader Australian society.
  3. Local Australians: Individuals interested in learning about and engaging with diverse international cultures present in their community, fostering cross-cultural understanding and connections.

Accessibility must be prioritised across all design and content decisions to ensure a seamless and equitable experience for every user, regardless of ability or the assistive technologies they may use.

Project Goals

The key goals for this website are:

  1. Authentic Representation & Cultural Celebration: Accurately represent the diverse experiences and identities of international students and migrants, avoiding stereotypes, while also showcasing cultural richness for interested local Australians.
  2. Accessible Information & Resource Hub: Provide clear, practical, and easily accessible information covering settlement challenges, employment pathways, cultural connections, and relevant community resources for both newcomers and locals seeking information.
  3. Meaningful Connections & Support Network: Foster connections among international students/migrants (peer support) and facilitate positive interactions between newcomers and the local community, strengthening social cohesion.
  4. Ethical, Inclusive & Sensitive Design: Ensure the website design, content, and features uphold the highest ethical standards, are inclusive of all users (considering language, ability, background), and demonstrate cultural sensitivity in all aspects.

Value 1: Promote Social Cohesion & Belonging

CCI aims to strengthen societal bonds and foster meaningful connections.

Need 1

Users need easy ways to find relevant local groups, events, and businesses to combat isolation.

Feature: Community Directory & Events

Searchable/filterable directory for groups & events. Allows user submissions. Meets need for connection. API: Events/Resource Directory.

Need 2

Users seek a platform to connect with peers, share practical advice, and offer mutual support.

Feature: Discussion Forum

Topic-based forum for peer Q&A and experience sharing. Fosters support & connection. API: Discussion Board.

Value 2: Empower Newcomers & Ensure Inclusivity

CCI prioritizes empowering diverse communities and ensuring inclusive platforms.

Need 3

Newcomers need a single, reliable, localized source for practical settlement info (housing, banking etc.)

Feature: Localized Settlement Guides

Section with practical guides for specific cities/regions. Peer input via forum possible. Provides key resources.

Need 4

Users desire access to critical/complex information (legal, health) in their native language for clarity.

Feature: Multi-language Content

Language switcher for key guides/navigation. Addresses major user need & promotes inclusivity.

Need 5

Users require website content in clear, simple English, avoiding jargon.

Feature: Plain English Strategy

Enforce Plain English guidelines (WCAG 3.1.5) for all content. Improves usability for everyone.

Need 6

Users need a trustworthy, moderated online space with clear purpose and privacy controls.

Feature: Moderation & Privacy Features

Implement clear guidelines, visible moderation, reporting tools, and user privacy settings. Builds trust.

Value 3: Celebrate & Maintain Cultural Identity

CCI aims to create platforms celebrating unique community identities.

Need 7

The website must authentically represent the community and avoid stereotypes.

Feature: Culturally Sensitive Content

Use authentic visuals/themes. Involve community in content review. Moderate user content for respect.

Need 8

Users seek specific cultural resources like specialty shops or cultural event listings.

Feature: Culturally Tagged Directory

Ensure directory includes categories/tags for cultural shops, services, and events. Aids cultural connection.

Value 4: Improve Quality of Life (Focus: Employment)

CCI aims to provide resources enhancing community members' lives, including employment opportunities.

Need 9

Users need localized job info, CV/interview help, networking tips, work culture insights, including support for partners.

Feature: Employment Support Hub

Section with curated job links, resume guides, networking tips, work culture info, and dedicated partner resources.

Value 5: Meet Core Technical & Ethical Requirements

CCI requires accessible, mobile-friendly, dynamic, and ethically designed websites.

Need 10

Website MUST comply with WCAG 2.2 Level AA accessibility standards.

Feature: Accessible Design Implementation

Apply WCAG AA (semantic HTML, contrast, keyboard nav, ARIA, alt text etc.). Test rigorously.

Need 11

Website must be responsive (mobile-first) and function as a PWA.

Feature: Responsive CSS & PWA Setup

Use mobile-first CSS (media queries, flex/grid). Implement Manifest & Service Worker for PWA.

Need 12

Must integrate one dynamic feature (e.g., Forum) using only the provided CCI APIs.

Feature: API Integration Module

Integrate chosen module (e.g., Discussion Board) using JS `Workspace` with specified CCI API endpoints.

Need 13

Mandatory integration of a third-party newsletter plugin (ethical concerns noted).

Feature: Styled Plugin Integration

Integrate plugin script. Apply CSS styling to match site design. Address ethics separately.
